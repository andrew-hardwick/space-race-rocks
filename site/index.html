The Rocket City Curling Club is excited to announce that we have been selected to host a qualifier for USA Curling's 5-and-Under National Championship. We will be hosting this event, titled "The Space Race" on April 1-3, 2022 in Huntsville, AL. This bonspiel is open to all USA Curling members with 5 or less years of eligibility (2020 does not count towards eligibility).

We will be opening registration on Saturday, December 4th at Noon (12:00 PM CST) with 32 team spots available on a first come first serve basis. The price is $400 per team ($360 for Rocket City Curling Club members). There is a 3 game guarantee for this event. We are encouraging all teams to have their members as we will not be filling teams with subs.
